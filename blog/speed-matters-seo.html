Why Speed Matters: The Hidden SEO Impact of Replying to Reviews

By Ben Linder • 4 min read

Every business owner knows 5-star reviews are good. But almost nobody talks about response time.

Google Loves Speed

Google's algorithm prioritizes active, engaged businesses. When you reply within 24 hours, you signal to Google that:

  • You are open and operating.
  • You care about customer service.
  • Your business listing is accurate and managed.

The "Freshness" Factor

Google has explicitly stated that replying to reviews improves your local ranking. A study by Moz found that review signals (quantity, velocity, diversity) make up over 15% of local pack ranking factors.

Don't Just Say "Thanks"

Generic replies like "Thanks!" are missed opportunities. Use keywords naturally in your response:

Good SEO Reply Example

"Thanks, Sarah! We're glad you enjoyed our [Specific Dish/Service]. Our team loves serving the best [Your Niche] in [City]. Hope to see you back soon!"
